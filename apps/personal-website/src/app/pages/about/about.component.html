About Me

In federal contracting you are assigned a labor category, one of which is a Journeyman. I've been a Journeyman in more ways than one.

I graduated from Duke University in 2018 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering. I learned MATLAB in one of the earliest classes in the cirriculum, and coupled with my Java experience from high school I decided to pursue a minor in CS. In the fall of 2018 I joined Booz Allen in Washington, D.C. as a Data Scientist working on NLP, optimization, and ML bias. For one of my projects, I built a front end in Flask and Python and loved thinking about user interaction and displaying information in effective ways. From that point on I've been building Single Page Applications in Angular, working closely with designers and clients make their visions a virtual reality.

So you can see it has been quite a journey, but I feel like I'm just getting started.
